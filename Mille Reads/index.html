What's it about?

In Her Own League by Liz Tomforde is a workplace romance following Reese Remington, the first female owner of the professional baseball team, the Windy City Warriors. While navigating a male-dominated industry and internal sabotage, she develops a deep connection with the team's protective manager, Emmett "Monty" Montgomery, forcing them to balance their intense professional friction with growing personal feelings.
